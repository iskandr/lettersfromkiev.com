August 29th, 1941

Hello dear Musitchka, Thanks God I am alive and healthy. Musenka, I have send you 3 telegrams and 10 letters and I do not have an answer. I am troubled. Musenka, is that so difficult to write a couple of words? Write how is your health and Yashaâ€™s health, You are not in good health. Do not worry and do not think about anything. Buy a warm coat and valenki for Yasha and galoshes for yourself. Musenka write me immediately with all the details. Send me a telegram right now. I worry a lot. Musenka, it is so painful to not have a single word about you and Yasha. You make me suffer and think a lot. Musya kiss Yasha (from me). Zina and Shura sent you Hello. As soon as you got this letter send me a telegram. Kisses to everybody. Write your address. Stay healthy, Yours truly Aron. RESPOND
