August 23rd, 1941

Hi Dear Musenka and my dear son Yashenka, I feel bad that I do not have any response from you. I sent you 2 telegrams and 5 letters and I do not know what to think, I worry a lot. Musinka ,My gold, please write me if you and Yasha are healthy. Do not worry about anything, eat and drink. God willing everything will be fine. I am longing for you, I am so lonesome. I hope to see you soon. Miron send you his hello, I am getting many letters from him. He works and doing well. Send him a letter:
Charkov,
Stalin Boulevard 199,
Chamber 36, 3-rd trade school.
Zakon M. A.
Greetings to all, Please respond, I missed you, Hugging you and Yasha. Stay healthy, Respond to me.
